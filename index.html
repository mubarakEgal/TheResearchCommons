The Research Commons, City St George's

The Research Commons runs structured research programmes for City St George's students. Participants work in small teams on mental health research projects led by an experienced researcher, with the goal of contributing to a peer-reviewed publication. All work is remote and designed to fit around your studies and other commitments. Students who contribute meaningfully are named as co-authors on the final paper.
